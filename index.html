First, What's Astrology ?

Studied for millennia, Astrology is a human science that establishes a correspondence between man and his environment (planets, stars, etc.). Based on countless observations made since the High Antiquity, Astrology makes it possible to determine certain psychological characteristics of a person according to planetary positions at the time of his birth.
